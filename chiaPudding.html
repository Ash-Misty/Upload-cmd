Chia Pudding with Berries

  • ingredients
  • instructions

Ingredients:-

  • 2 tbsp chia seeds
  • ½ cup milk (dairy, almond, or coconut)
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • ¼ cup mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 tbsp chopped nuts (optional, for crunch)

Instructions:-

  1. In a jar or bowl, mix chia seeds, milk, honey, and vanilla extract.
  2. Let it sit for 5 minutes, then stir again to prevent clumping.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  4. Before eating, top with fresh berries and nuts.
