Granulo Bar

  • ingredients
  • instructions

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • ½ cup nuts (almonds, walnuts, cashews- chopped)
  • ¼ cup honey or maple syrup
  • ¼ cup peanut butter (or almond butter)
  • ¼ cup dried fruits (raisins, cranberries, dates- chopped)
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract (optional)
  • A pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Toast oats & nuts (optional): Dry roast oats and nuts in a pan for 3–4 minutes until slightly golden.
  2. Mix wet ingredients: In a bowl, mix honey and peanut butter. Microwave for 20 seconds to soften, then stir well.
  3. Combine: Add oats, nuts, dried fruits, vanilla extract, and salt. Mix until everything is well coated.
  4. Set in a pan: Line a small tray or dish with parchment paper. Press the mixture firmly into the pan.
  5. Chill: Refrigerate for at least 1 hour until firm.
  6. Slice into bars and store in an airtight container.
